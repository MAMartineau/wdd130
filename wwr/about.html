Our Commitment to an Exciting and Safe Experience

Our mission is to provide the most exciting and safe rafting experiences, where adventure meets peace of mind. We strive to ensure every guest feels the thrill of the rapids while knowing they’re in the hands of highly skilled guides who prioritize both fun and safety. Our goal is to create unforgettable moments that inspire a love for adventure and the great outdoors.

Our History

Rafting Adventurers was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate adventurers who shared a love for the outdoors and a thirst for excitement. What began as a small team of experienced river guides quickly grew into a renowned outfitting company. From the wild rapids of the Colorado River to the challenging white waters of the Zambezi, we’ve navigated some of the world’s toughest and most exhilarating rivers.
