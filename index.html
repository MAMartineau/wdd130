Utah

Utah, located in the western United States, is known for its stunning natural landscapes, including national parks like Zion, Bryce Canyon, and Arches. The state features diverse geography, from red rock deserts to alpine forests. Its capital, Salt Lake City, is a hub for industry, culture, and outdoor recreation. Utah is also recognized for its rich pioneer history and is home to a significant population of members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The state offers a mix of urban growth and vast, scenic wilderness.
